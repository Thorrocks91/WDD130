Sweet Treats Made to Order

One of our most frequent questions has been how fast is our shipping process. We take our shipping process very seriously. We want our customers to have the best-tasting treats. Orders are set to arrive on time and are typically shipped 1-3 days before your selected date. You will receive a tracking email when the item/items have shipped. Sometimes you may receive an email letting us know that we are making your cake and we are about to send it out for advance notice. All our products will be packaged in special containers to keep your items cool. We will ensure your treats are safe during the delivery process and guarantee freshness upon delivery. All of our treats will come ready to eat. We only recommend you keep our treats for 4-5 days. After that, the items should be thrown out or put in the freezer by day the second day to preserve shelf life. If you ever need any help with an online delivery, please contact us by email: TMaries@sweettreats.com or you can call us Monday through Friday MST at 1-800-TMARIES(687-4377)
