How to Pipe Scalloped Edging

The optimum tip for piping the scales is a large round tip, however I frequently use a piping bag with the tip chopped off for this purpose. Position the bag so that the seams are on the side of the cake rather than in the line of sight this will save time. To create the two-tone look, fill one side of the piping bag with one color of buttercream and the other with another buttercream, alternating between the two colors. Colors that are very similar should be combined for the greatest impact. Set aside a little amount of uncolored buttercream. You may use the conserved white to brighten any of the colored batches, or you can mix them together.
